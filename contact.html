FAQs

Common Questions

Who can join Rotaract Versova?

Young people between the ages of 18 and 30 who are passionate about community service and leadership are welcome to join.

Is there a membership fee?

There is a nominal annual membership fee as per Rotary International guidelines. Contact us to learn more.

How often do you hold events?

We run a weekly food donation drive every Sunday and conduct multiple service projects throughout the year spanning a variety of causes.

Can organizations collaborate with you?

Absolutely! We're always open to partnerships with NGOs, corporates, and institutions for CSR and community service collaborations. Reach out via the form above.
