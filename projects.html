Milestone

Meeting with the District Governor

We marked an important milestone by receiving our official certificate from District Governor Mr. Manish Motwani.

Environment · Tree Planting

Celebrating World Rotaract Day

We celebrated by planting saplings with enthusiastic local children to give back to the planet.

Food & Nutrition

Project Annadaan Mahadaan

We celebrated Holi by distributing wholesome South Indian meals to the street children of Azad Nagar.

Community Engagement

StreetSmile PlayDate

We spent a meaningful afternoon organizing engaging games and teaching basic fitness to children in a Vera Desai slum.

Club Bonding

Movie Night

We bonded as a club over snacks and a private screening of Mean Girls.

Community

Smile Saathi

We organized a fun-filled dance party and games for children with learning disabilities at Anandi Special School.

Sports & Fun

Mini Olympics 1.0

We hosted an energetic sports event featuring three main challenges.

  • Three in One Challenge
  • Triple Turf Cup
  • Speed Shuffle
Donation

Project Aashray

We collected and distributed over 6,000 articles of clothing to 1,105 students at a tribal school in Parali.

Health & Hygiene

Project Sahaara (Gem Busters)

We conducted interactive hygiene awareness activities for children and distributed comprehensive personal care kits.

Education

Project Udaan

We hosted an inspiring movie screening with snacks for underprivileged children at Gundecha Academy.

Recurring · Every Sunday

Weekly Food Donation

Every Sunday, we partner with Rotary of Mumbai Versova to distribute food and treats to children in the Azad Nagar slums.
